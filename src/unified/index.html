About 24Dash

24Dash is a website that has apps made by PilotScythe and GreenvillSSnick (with other people as contributed). It has a pilot client and controller client respectively, each doing things for each respective.

The pilot client, 24Flight, is based on Flightradar24, Navigraph Charts, Chartfox, Foreflight, Garmin Pilot, Simbrief, and Windy.
